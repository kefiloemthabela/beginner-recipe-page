Cinnamon rolls

A cinnamon roll (also known as cinnamon bun, cinnamon swirl, cinnamon scroll, cinnamon Danish and cinnamon snail) is a sweet roll commonly served in Northern Europe and North America.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Whole milk
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Instant yeast
  • Vanilla Essence
  • Cream cheese
  • Powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together in a large bowl
  2. Warm the milk and butter together in a seperate bowl
  3. Whisk in the yeast until it has dissolved
  4. Pour the mixture over the dry ingredients
  5. Add eggs and mix everything together
  6. Transfer the dough to a work surface
  7. Knead by hand for 3 minutes until a soft dough forms
  8. Let the dough rest for 10 minutes

Ingredient filling

  • Softened butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough into a large rectangle
  2. Mix the brown sugar and cinnamon
  3. Spread the butter onto the dough
  4. Sprinkle the sugar mixture on the butter
  5. Roll the dough into a log
  6. Cut into equal pieces
  7. Arrange in a greased pan
  8. Let the rolls rise until double the size
  9. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown

Icing

Tip:Use a premium instant yeast, which cuts down on rise time
